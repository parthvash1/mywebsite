MUSEUM OF CHOCOLATE

In our museum, you can journey back to the origins of chocolate and experience how cocoa conquered europe and then during the world war came to india. Meet the Swiss chocolate pioneers who made our lives sweeter with their inventions. . And find out just how irresistible chocolate is for yourselves.

MUSEUM OF CHOCOLATE

In our Chocolate museum, spread over more than 500 m2, you will find the best chocolates, pralines and a whole range of other delicious treats. Design your own personal praline packaging and have a Master Chocolatier create a chocolate bar exactly to your wishes.

MUSEUM OF CHOCOLATE

The Chocolate museum welcomes visitors with a highlight towering over them at over nine meters tall: the most impressive chocolate fountain in the world. The seemingly weightless construction holds 1,500 kg of real chocolate and is the perfect start to the incomparable experience at the Chocolate museum . See it for yourself and experience the fantastic chocolate fountain in the chocolate museum.
